IFCE-RICE-SENAI 100K PROGRAM

Artificial Intelligence for Urban Sustainability and Resilience to Natural Disasters in the Americas

Using Artificial Intelligence to improve the sustainability and resistance of cities to natural disasters is a fundamental step to guarantee safety and quality of life for people. The IFCE-RICE-SENAI 100K Program provides innovative solutions that help us face these challenges.

About

The IFCE-Rice-SENAI Program on Artificial Intelligence for Urban Sustainability and Resilience to NaturalDisasters in the Americas is a tripartite alliance integrating research and development (R&D), education, andlocal partnerships to improve infrastructure systems to better handle hydroclimate extremes. This is a US-Brazilcollaboration between the following institutions:
  • Rice University(Rice)
  • Instituto FederalEducação, Ciência e Tecnologia do Ceará (IFCE)
  • Instituto SENAI de Inovação de Recife (SENAI)

Funding

This program is one of the winners of the 2021 U.S.-Brazil Innovation Fund Grant Competition, which is supported by:
  • The United States Department of State
  • Brazil’s National Service of Industrial Training (SENAI)
  • The Partner of the Americas
